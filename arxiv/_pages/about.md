I am a Marie Curie Fellow in the [Machine Materials Lab](https://coulaislab.com/) at the University of Amsterdam. My interests are in soft matter science, with a focus on [elasticity](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/sciadv.abk3079), [geometry](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.125.047801) and [active, bio-inspired systems](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.124.088001).

Previously, I was a postdoc with the [Souslov Lab](https://people.bath.ac.uk/as3764/author/jack-binysh/). I defended my PhD thesis, [Construction and Dynamics of Knotted Fields in Soft Matter Systems](https://github.com/jackbinysh/Thesis) in 2019 with [Gareth Alexander](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/physics/staff/academic/galexander/) at the Centre for Complexity Science, University of Warwick.
